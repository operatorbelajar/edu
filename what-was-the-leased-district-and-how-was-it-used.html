What Was The Leased District And How Was It Used

March 03 2023 oleh rindi9568

? The Leased District was an area of the Choctaw Nation in what is now southeastern Oklahoma. It was the first area of Indian Territory to be formally leased to non-Native Americans, with the Choctaw Nation leasing much of the area to the Federal government in 1866. The leased district was used mainly for railroads, with the Federal government eventually leasing much of the land to railroad companies. The railroads helped to open up the West, as they provided a means of transportation for settlers, goods and services.
