Nadine Gordimer Wrote Many Stories About The Injustices Of Apartheid

March 03 2023 oleh helenaludong

Nadine Gordimer was a South African author and anti-apartheid activist who wrote extensively about the injustices of apartheid. Her stories shed light on the struggles of South Africans who were oppressed under the system of racial segregation and discrimination. She won the Nobel Prize in Literature in 1991 for her work. Her stories often focus on individuals or families struggling against the oppressive system of apartheid and the many injustices it caused. Through her stories, Gordimer was able to give voice to the struggles of those who were oppressed and show the world the true nature of apartheid.

Jawaban:

Listening to stories has numerous advantages, including:

1.Improved language and vocabulary: Hearing stories helps people expand their vocabulary and improve their language skills, especially in children.

2.Emotional development: Stories can evoke strong emotions, allowing people to experience and process their feelings, and to develop empathy and compassion.

3.Improved memory: Listening to stories can help with memory recall and retention.

4.Cultural Awareness: Listening to stories from different cultures can broaden people's perspectives and increase cultural understanding and awareness.

5.Imagination and creativity: Stories encourage the imagination and can help foster creativity.

6.Relaxation and stress relief: Listening to stories can be a form of entertainment and relaxation, helping to reduce stress and anxiety.

7.Moral and ethical development: Many stories contain lessons, values, and moral messages that can shape people's behavior and understanding of right and wrong.

8.Bonding and social connection: Sharing stories with others can build bonds, promote empathy, and create a sense of community and connection.

30. the life of benjamin franklin, a practical man ________ many stories have been told, was unusually productive.


Kata yang tepat untuk melengkapi kalimat di atas adalah about whom.

The life of Benjamin Franklin, a practical man about whom many stories have been told, was unusually productive.

Pembahasan:

Soal tersebut membahas mengenai Tata Bahasa atau yang biasa disebut Grammar. Grammar dapat diartikan sebagai struktur dalam menyusun kalimat. Dengan menggunakan struktur yang benar, sebuah kalimat akan menjadi sempurna.

Dari soal di atas, kalimat itu merupakan klausa kata sifat, yang menggunakan kata ganti relatif yang menunjukkan manusia diperlukan. Kata 'about whom' berisi kata ganti relatif (yang) yang tidak merujuk pada kata benda manusia.
